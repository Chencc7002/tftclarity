2. What the Service provides

tftclarity helps Chinese-speaking Teamfight Tactics players understand aggregated game data through natural-language queries. Features may include:

  • Champion item builds and individual item comparisons.
  • Composition rankings and patch trends.
  • Sample counts, average placement, Top 4 rate, win rate, and data-quality warnings.

  • Multi-turn follow-up queries that retain anonymous short-lived context.
