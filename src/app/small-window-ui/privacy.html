Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 20, 2026

Plain-language summary. tftclarity can be used without an account. We do not ask for a Riot ID, do not use Riot Sign On, do not sell personal information, and do not use advertising trackers. We process anonymous technical identifiers, queries, settings, and optional feedback only to provide, protect, and improve the service.

This Privacy Policy explains how the independent tftclarity project (“tftclarity,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use tftclarity.cn and its related web application (the “Service”). The Service is operated from the People’s Republic of China.

1. Information we process

CategoryExamples and purposeTypical retention
Anonymous visitor identifier A signed tft_visitor cookie is used for abuse prevention, request limits, anonymous settings, and separating one visitor’s conversation state from another’s. It does not contain a name, email address, or Riot identifier. Cookie lifetime: up to 1 year. Daily quota counters are organized by day.
Queries and result context The text you submit, a random browser-generated conversation ID, selected filters, structured query data, result metadata, and whether an AI-generated explanation was used. Server-side query snapshots: up to 30 days. Query cache: about 5 minutes. Conversation context: about 30 minutes.
Preferences and local settings Rank filters, sample thresholds, item scope, language, settings-panel state, and layout width. Some preferences are stored server-side under an anonymous scope; language and layout choices are stored in your browser’s local storage. Until reset, cleared, or no longer needed.
Optional feedback If you select “Helpful,” “Not helpful,” or save an alias candidate, we store the related query, normalized result details, rating, and a random feedback ID so we can diagnose quality issues. Until the feedback is reviewed, deleted, or no longer needed for product improvement.
Security and service logs Request time, requested path, browser user agent, network address, error details, and hashed network/visitor identifiers used for rate limiting, security, troubleshooting, and availability. Raw infrastructure logs follow the hosting system’s configured rotation. Security records are kept only as long as reasonably needed.

Please do not place names, contact details, Riot account credentials, API keys, or other sensitive information in a query or feedback message.

2. Information we do not request

  • No tftclarity account is required.
  • We do not currently use Riot Sign On and do not ask for a Riot ID, PUUID, match history, or ranked profile belonging to a specific user.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not intentionally identify, profile, or analyze individual players.
  • We do not sell personal information or share it for cross-site behavioral advertising.

3. How we use information

  • Provide natural-language TFT queries, aggregated statistics, and follow-up conversation context.
  • Remember your anonymous preferences and maintain fair usage limits.
  • Generate structured parsing or evidence-grounded explanations when optional AI features are enabled.
  • Investigate errors, prevent abuse, secure the Service, and improve data and answer quality.
  • Comply with applicable law and enforce our Terms of Service.

Depending on the context, our processing is based on providing the Service you request, your choice to submit optional feedback, our legitimate interest in operating a secure and useful service, and compliance with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and browser storage

Strictly necessary cookie

tft_visitor is an HTTP-only, same-site cookie used to distinguish anonymous visitors, enforce request limits, and isolate settings and conversation state. In production it is transmitted only over HTTPS. It expires after up to one year. Because this cookie is necessary to protect and operate the public Service, disabling it may prevent some features from working correctly.

Local storage

The browser stores language, panel, and layout preferences under keys beginning with tftagent.. These values stay on your device until you clear site data or reset them in the application.

We do not currently use third-party advertising or cross-site analytics cookies.

5. Service providers and data sources

We may disclose limited information to service providers only where needed to run the Service:

  • Hosting and security providers process network and service logs to deliver and protect the site.
  • Configured OpenAI-compatible AI providers may receive the query and structured evidence required to parse or explain a result when an optional AI feature is enabled. The product remains usable with deterministic, non-AI fallbacks.
  • TFT data providers receive structured game-data requests. The current prototype uses third-party aggregated MetaTFT statistics and official/static TFT sources. We do not intentionally send direct user identifiers with those requests.

These providers may process information in other countries. We use only the minimum information reasonably needed for the feature and require providers to handle it under their own security and privacy obligations.

6. Data security

We use HTTPS, HTTP-only signed visitor cookies, server-side secrets, scoped anonymous identifiers, access controls, request limits, and data minimization. Riot API keys and AI provider keys are intended to remain on the server and are not included in browser code. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices and rights

You can clear browser storage through your browser, reset application preferences, clear the current conversation, stop submitting feedback, or stop using the Service. Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or a copy of personal information associated with you.

Because the Service is anonymous, we may be unable to connect a record to you without additional details. When making a request, include the approximate date and time, the query or feedback concerned, and—only if you are comfortable doing so—the anonymous visitor cookie value shown in your browser’s developer tools. Do not post this information publicly.

Send privacy requests to tftclarity@outlook.com. We may ask for reasonable verification and will respond as required by applicable law.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, or the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. A parent or guardian who believes a child provided personal information may contact us for deletion.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the Service, providers, or legal requirements change. We will change the “last updated” date and, for material changes, provide a reasonable notice on the site.
